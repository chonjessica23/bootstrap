I think pizza is great because there is literally nothing wrong about it. It's everyones go-to dish! Why? Because there's a type of pizza for anyone and everyone to enjoy! My personal favorite is a margherita slice with some nice red pepper flakes on it and/or tabasco sauce. But since it's pizza, so any slice is a good slice.
